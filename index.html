About Yekta

I’m a frontend-focused software developer with 5+ years across web apps, APIs, and AI-enabled workflows. I love joining projects early, shaping architecture, and then staying close to the details: tests, accessibility, performance, and those tiny UX decisions that make an interface feel kind.

These days I split my energy between building smooth, testable experiences with Next.js/React/Vue and designing AI/ML workflows that make products smarter—not more complicated. I thrive in fast-moving teams where we ship thoughtfully and document as we go.

When I’m not coding, I’m exploring design ideas, reading about AI, or wandering the city with a camera and a coffee.
